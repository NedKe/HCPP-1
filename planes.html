Dynamic WT-9

Dynamic is a beautiful, beautiful aircraft that provides a wonderful flying experience! There are 2 Dynamic in the club and SUF has owned more over the years.Dynamic was acquired for the first time in 2006 and the club was at the forefront of a state-of-the-art ultralight aircraft. Since 2013, it has been the club's primary school flight and it is much appreciated by students. WT9 Dynamic is a state-of-the-art high-performance aircraft built from carbon fiber, fiberglass and Kevlar.The engine is a Rotax 912 ULS with an output of 100 hp. The aircraft's cruising speed is about 120 kts and Vne is about 160 kts. Our Dynamic are also equipped with a portable GPS - EKP IV, with 7 inch screen based on Jeppesen database, and 2-axis autopilot.

Skylane

Klubbens Skylane v.5 is a high-winged ultra-light aircraft of traditional design. The plane is constructed entirely in composite with a rescue parachute and integrated wing tanks of 47 liters each, ie. a total of 94 liters. Skylanen has steering wheel steering that allows easy entry and exit, which, especially at sea, is positive.The flap is electric and very efficient, which allows landings on short fields. The trim is also electric.The plane has toe tip brakes on both sides and a free-swinging nose wheel that allows very narrow turns, for example on the track. Skylanen is very easy to fly and has nice stable properties.The club's Skylane has an 80hp Rotax engine that gives it a cruise speed of 97 kt at 4800 rpm and then the fuel consumption is about 14 liters / hour

Skylane as seaplane

The Skylan is also supplemented with aluminum floats of the Fillinger type and can be a very practical seaplane with a large easily accessible cargo space. The club's Skylane is also equipped with water-borne carburetor preheating and an oil thermostat which leads to faster heating of the engine and a more even oil temperature.
