We train you from start to UL certificate

Training begins in double command, but one fine day you sit there, it's time for your first solo flight (We pilots call it E.K.). We promise to train you as a safe pilot. We also promise that you will never be perfect! An aviator learns all his life. Our theory lessons contain a wide variety of topics such as engine science, flight rules, navigation, meteorology and aviation medicine to name a few examples. Our experienced instructors will make you a pilot in some of the most modern aircraft available. The training takes place on Dynamic and on our new Skylane. You can start by theory courses and pair with an instructor to start flight training at the same time. Theory course start every year in first of October and ends by end of March the next year. There are eight subjects within the course and you write an exam at the end of each subject while one of our teachers with be supervising the exam. In order to be able to fly with an instructor you need a flight medical certificate obtained from one of the authorised medical doctors. You also need student permit from transport office authorities before you can start your flight training. You need to buy a yearly membership to be able to book flights with your instructor.
